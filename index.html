Welcome!

Autocare Hub is your one-stop shop for advice on all your vehicle maintenance needs. Keep track of your vehicles upkeep, look for new ways to save money on vehicle maintenance, and contact mechanics all from this website.

Drivers

The basic user profile allows you to track your vehicles in your profile. You can record what work has been done to your vehicle and any details about the vehicle that you want to provide and then the system will prompt you to do basic maintenance tasks automatically based on that information. As a premium user, you can use the "Get Quote" page inside the profile page to send your ticket out to the wider market of mechanics and then choose the quote that best matches your expectations.

Mechanics

The mechanic profile is a special type of user profile that can provide specific support for users’ vehicles. As a mechanic, you can provide quotes to users based on the information they provide you, bid for specific quotes requests, view your list of jobs for the day, and notify customers about upcoming maintenance needs for their vehicles.

Workshops

The workshop profile is catered to businesses that want to implement the AutoCare Hub at the company level. Many of the features can be changed to better suit a company’s approach to car maintenance. Please contact us for details.
